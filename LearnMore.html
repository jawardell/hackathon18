This is an invitation to our semi annual Recycle Party!

FOOD!! DRINKS!! GAMES!! FUN!!

Where?

The event will take place from 5pm-7pm on the Front Lawn. This occasion is open to the Valdosta community, not just students.

Activities

  • Corn Hole
  • Ring Toss
  • Moon Walk
  • Obstacle Course

How to earn Prizes

Attendees can bring recyclables to our recycling booth and we will reward those with points to add to their profile. The more you bring, the better the benefits e.g. coupons for the VSU bookstore, t-shirts, waivers for FREE FOOD at the gathering, etc.

Take the time to stop by and learn about preserving the environmet. Invite friends and family to come out and have fun. Hope to see you there!
