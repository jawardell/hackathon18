Some useful tips on how to reduce, reuse, and recycle:

  1. Instead of using plastic, use glass jars, containers, and/ or metal/ reusable water bottles.
  2. Recycle some water usage for your house plants.
  3. Encourage an artist! They can use the recycled items to build things like a house or any other resources like a solar panel.
  4. Instead of paper, we can digitally document everything we need on day to day life.
  5. When going grocery shopping, use a reusable bag that you bring yourself instead of the plastic bags they have there.
